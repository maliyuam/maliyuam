About Me

I'm a graduate researcher at Carnegie Mellon University with a focus on AI Governance, entrepreneurship journeys in Africa, and the application of technology for economic and social development. With a multidisciplinary background spanning Computer Science, Information Technology, and community leadership, I work at the intersection of technology and social impact.

Currently pursuing my MSc. in Information Technology at Carnegie Mellon University and studying AI Governance at BlueDot Impact, I'm driven by a passion to ensure that emerging technologies serve humanity in safe, ethical, and beneficial ways.

As the Founding President of Africa Innovators Society, I've led initiatives that empower students to address pressing challenges through innovation, impacting over 300 community members and generating 15+ entrepreneurial solutions.
